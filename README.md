Rangkui

Rangkui is an open-source project developed by SetiadiFOSS to support the development of web-based information systems and applications based on the principles of clean architecture, open collaboration, and community-driven innovation.
This repository serves as an open collaboration space for developers, researchers, and students who want to contribute to the growth of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) in Indonesia.

Objectives:

Promote the adoption of open-source technology in educational and governmental environments.

Provide a foundational codebase that can be extended for various information system needs.

Encourage community learning and contributions to FOSS development.
